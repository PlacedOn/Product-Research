# Competitive Analysis

## Landscape Overview

The AI hiring space has several players, but none combine adaptive conversational assessment with Markovian reasoning and built-in intersectional bias detection.

## Our Moat (Why We're Hard to Copy)

### Technical Moat

1. **Markovian reasoning architecture** — not trivial to implement. Requires understanding of state management, DAG decomposition, and contraction. Most competitors use simple prompt chains.

2. **Atomic trait convergence** — the progressive refinement toward irreducible behavioral insights is a property of the architecture, not something you can bolt on.

3. **ABLEIST bias detection** — grounded in disability studies literature with validated metrics. Not just "we check for bias" but a rigorous 8-metric framework validated by domain experts.

### Data Moat (Emerging)

4. **Interview-to-outcome correlation data** — once you track which profiles lead to successful hires, this data becomes your moat. No one else has it.

5. **Atomic trait taxonomy** — over time, you'll build a library of validated atomic traits mapped to job performance. This becomes a proprietary asset.

### Network Moat (Long-term)

6. **"Interview once, apply everywhere"** — candidates want to be where employers are, and vice versa. Classic marketplace network effect.

## Indian Market Opportunity vs. Competitors

PlacedOn has structural advantages in India specifically:

**Market Gap:**
- 1.5M engineers graduate annually in India with weak resumes and no work experience
- Existing incumbents (HireVue, Mercor) target established enterprises or contract talent
- Campus hiring and referral networks dominate — no AI tool has cracked this yet
- Cost sensitivity in India makes $299/month appealing vs. $25K+ HireVue or Mercor's contract-focused model

**Our Positioning in India:**
| Factor | HireVue | Mercor | PlacedOn |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cost | $25K+/year | Per-hire or SaaS (higher) | $299/month |
| Adaptive interview | No (pre-recorded) | Maybe | Yes (real-time) |
| Target market | Large enterprises | Contract talent | Startups + tier 2-3 colleges |
| Fresher-friendly | No | Unclear | Yes — designed for freshers |
| Bias framework | Generic moderation | Unclear | ABLEIST 8-metric (validated) |
| Indian accent handling | Unknown | Unknown | Priority (Hinglish support) |

No existing player combines:

- Atomic behavioral trait profiling (irreducible, actionable insights)
- Intersectional bias detection (ABLEIST framework, validated)
- $0.35–0.50 per interview cost with voice (100x cheaper than HireVue)
- Built for the Indian fresher market specifically

This combination is our positioning. The market gap is real and large.
