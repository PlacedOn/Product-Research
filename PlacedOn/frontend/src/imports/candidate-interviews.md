# Candidate Interviews Page

Route: `/candidate/interviews`

## Goal

Give candidates one place to manage profile interviews, company intros, skill refresh sessions, and past sessions.

## Required Sections

1. Header
   - Title: `Interviews`
   - Subtitle: `Manage your PlacedOn interview, company intros, and skill refresh sessions.`
   - CTA: `Start New Interview`
   - Secondary CTA: `Practice Text Mode`

2. Summary cards
   - Profile interview
   - Company intros
   - Skill refresh

3. Upcoming section
   - Company intro cards
   - Skill refresh cards
   - Actions: confirm, reschedule, decline, start refresh, remind later

4. Profile interview status
   - Variants: not started, in progress, completed, review pending
   - Timeline: started, warm-up completed, technical scenario completed, deep follow-up completed, profile generated, review pending

5. Past interviews
   - Interview, role, date, outcome, action

6. Preparation panel
   - Quiet place
   - Camera and mic ready
   - Text mode available
   - Pause anytime
   - No trick questions

7. Trust card
   - `Company interviews and profile refreshes never change your public profile without review.`

## Responsive Behavior

Desktop: main content plus prep/trust side panel.

Mobile: stacked sections, vertical timeline, interview rows become cards.
