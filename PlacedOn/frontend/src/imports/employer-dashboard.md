# Employer Dashboard

Route: `/employer`

## Goal

Create a focused employer workspace for discovering candidates by verified interview evidence instead of resume keywords.

## Required Sections

1. Header
   - Label: `Employer workspace`
   - Title: `Evidence-backed candidate discovery`
   - Metrics: open jobs, saved candidates, intro requests

2. Job tabs or sidebar
   - Active jobs
   - Selected job controls feed relevance

3. Trait filter bar
   - Search
   - Filters: role, level, skills, traits, location, availability

4. Candidate feed
   - Anonymous candidate cards
   - Role fit percentage
   - Top traits
   - Verified skills
   - Interview freshness
   - Actions: `Request Intro`, `Save`, `Pass`

5. Trust/privacy panel
   - Processed profile, not raw transcript
   - Skills grounded in interview evidence
   - Identity hidden until candidate approval
   - Contact requires two-way opt-in

6. Saved shortlist panel
   - Compact list or empty state

## Visual Direction

Employer pages should be more data-dense and restrained than candidate pages. Use the same brand system, but prioritize fast scanning and decision clarity.

## Responsive Behavior

Desktop: job sidebar, candidate feed, trust/saved side panel.

Mobile: feed-first layout, filters collapse into drawer, candidate cards show essentials first.
